# Ptolemy App — Daily Transit Interpretations
## 49 combinations: transiting planet × natal planet
### Source: Vettius Valens, Anthologies · Claudius Ptolemy, Tetrabiblos
### Style: 2-3 sentences, direct, practical, traditional voice

---

## Framework

Each entry describes what it means when a transiting planet aspects a natal planet.
The aspect type (conjunction, trine, sextile, square, opposition) modifies the tone:
- Conjunction / Trine / Sextile: use the base text as written (favorable framing)
- Square / Opposition: prefix with "With friction," and shift tone toward challenge

The Moon section is displayed separately as a daily card at the top.

# TRANSITING SUN

**Transiting Sun ☌ △ ⚹ natal Sun**
The Sun returns to or harmonizes with its natal position, illuminating the native's core identity and vital force. This is a period of natural confidence and clarity — the solar principle is reinforced, and efforts toward recognition and authority tend to be well-received. Energy levels are typically high, and the will is clear.

**Transiting Sun ☌ △ ⚹ natal Moon**
The solar principle touches the lunar — the conscious will and the emotional life come into alignment. Daily routines feel purposeful, and the native finds it easier than usual to act from a place of genuine feeling rather than obligation. Public circumstances and private needs are briefly in accord.

**Transiting Sun ☌ △ ⚹ natal Mercury**
The Sun illuminates Mercury's domain of intelligence and communication. Ideas come with unusual clarity, and the native's words carry more authority than usual. A favorable period for writing, speaking, negotiating, or any work requiring the mind to operate at its best.

**Transiting Sun ☌ △ ⚹ natal Venus**
The solar principle touches Venus, brightening the native's social and pleasurable life. Relationships feel warmer and more generous, and the native tends to make a particularly favorable impression on others. A good period for matters of love, art, and the pursuit of beauty.

**Transiting Sun ☌ △ ⚹ natal Mars**
The Sun activates Mars, intensifying the native's drive, physical energy, and competitive instincts. Decisive action is favored, and the native tends to pursue goals with unusual focus and force. The key is to direct this energy toward genuine achievement rather than unnecessary conflict.

**Transiting Sun ☌ △ ⚹ natal Jupiter**
The Sun touches Jupiter, expanding the native's sense of confidence and good fortune. Opportunities for recognition and advancement tend to appear, and the native's efforts in professional and public life carry an air of natural authority. A period of genuine optimism and expanding horizons.

**Transiting Sun ☌ △ ⚹ natal Saturn**
The Sun meets Saturn, bringing matters of discipline, responsibility, and long-term structure into focus. The native is called to take stock of what has been built and what still requires patient effort. What is undertaken seriously now tends to produce lasting results.

Saturn meets the solar principle, calling the native to a serious reckoning with identity, authority, and long-term direction. **Transiting Saturn ☌ △ ⚹ natal Moon**

**Transiting Saturn ☌ △ ⚹ natal Venus**
Saturn meets Venus, bringing seriousness and endurance to the native's relationships and aesthetic life. Long-term commitments feel particularly meaningful and are well-supported during this period.

**Transiting Saturn ☌ △ ⚹ natal Mars**

Saturn meets Jupiter, bringing realistic assessment to the native's philosophical optimism and expansive ambitions. **Transiting Saturn ☌ △ ⚹ natal Saturn**

The native is called to a deep reckoning with the structures, commitments, and responsibilities that define their life. What has been built with genuine effort and integrity endures; what has not is revealed clearly.
